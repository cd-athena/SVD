SVD: Spatial Video Dataset

A comprehensive collection of spatial video data designed for research and development in computer vision and spatial computing. Our dataset provides high-quality spatial video recordings that enable researchers to explore and develop new algorithms for spatial computing applications.

Key Features:

  • High-resolution spatial video recordings
  • Multiple camera angles and perspectives
  • Rich metadata and annotations
  • Comprehensive documentation
